What this is

Compiled Thoughts is a research log. Not a tutorial site. Not a product. Just notes from someone trying to run the largest possible open-weight AI model on hardware anyone can buy.

The goal: DeepSeek V4 Flash (284B parameters, 13B active) at 20 tokens/second on an RTX 3070 Ti. The RTX 3070 Ti is the most common serious gaming GPU in the world. If it works there, it works for millions of people.

All experiments are real. All numbers are measured. Nothing is theoretical.

  • llama.cpp / BeeLLaMA.cpp
  • CUDA 12.8 · Blackwell FP4
  • DFloat11 lossless compression
  • TurboQuant KV cache
  • MTP speculative decoding
  • Custom MoE inference engine (in progress)
