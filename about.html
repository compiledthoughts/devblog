About

CompiledThoughts.dev is a public build log with one goal: run the largest possible open-weight AI model on consumer hardware, one click.

Open-weight models are getting bigger and better fast. But actually running them — on your own machine, without cloud APIs or expensive subscriptions — is still harder than it should be. This blog is about closing that gap.

Every post is a step in the build: benchmarks, tooling, configuration, failures, and breakthroughs. Written for developers who want to understand what’s actually happening under the hood.
